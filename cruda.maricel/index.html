NFT Locator app offers a way to enable you to track and record NFT assets printed and displayed anywhere in the world. (i.e., Tokenframe, digital frames and signage, printed posters, custom luxury purses, billboards, and Digital art galleries). You'll be able to take photos and create data list of your favorites and new NFT assets. It will be a great resources for any artists, designers, blockchain investors and NFT art enthusiasts.
