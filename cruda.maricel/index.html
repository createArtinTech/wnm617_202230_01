NFT Artwork Locator

Welcome to NFT Artwork locator. This app will enable you to track and record NFT artworks that you'll see in any physical environment (i.e., subways, smart watch, backpacks, buses, billboards, digital galleries etc.). You'll be able to take photos and create data list of your favorite NFT digital artworks. This app will be a great resources for any artist, designers, blockchain investors and NFT art enthusiasts.
